Account and Profile Management

Users can manage their accounts in the following ways:

  • Add Profile Picture: You can click on the profile picture to add your own photos.
  • Add Account: By clicking "Add Account," you can add your friends to the app simply by knowing their name, email, and password.
  • Edit Profile: You can click "My Profile" to give your friends a name you like.
  • Delete Account: If you want to delete an account, you can click "Delete" to remove it.
  • Change Password: By clicking "My Profile," you can change the username and password you use to log in.

Contact and Suggestions

  • Contact: If you have any suggestions for us, you can click on "Contact." Please make sure to provide your name and email correctly when submitting a suggestion.

Usage and Quiz

  • Main Page: On the Main Page, you can read about topics like Programmer Language, Football Sports, General Knowledge, Buddha, Famous Persons, and Health and Food.
  • Quiz: After studying these topics, you can take a "Quiz" to test how much you have understood.

Logging Out

  • Settings: The Setting menu is currently used only for logging out.
