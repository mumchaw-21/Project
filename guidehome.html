Account Creation and Usage Guide



  1. For those without an account, click "signup" at the top right of the page.


  2. For those who already have an account, click "Deploy now" .
  3. For users signing up, accurately fill out the signup form.


  4. When the box appears saying "signup successful! You can now login." , click "OK".


  5. The login form will appear. Enter the name and password exactly as you did during signup. When login successful appears , click "OK".


  6. Once you reach the menu shown in image , you can read "About us" and start using the app.
